About CARDET

CARDET (Center for the Advancement of Research and Development in Educational Technology) is the leading independent research and education centre in Cyprus and one of the prominent ones in the Euro-Mediterranean region. We design, develop, and implement innovative, evidence-based approaches to build competencies of individuals and communities.

Mission

Inspiring next generation education, and promoting research, innovation and development.

Vision

A just and sustainable world, where people learn and flourish.

💎
Values
Excellence
Highest quality research, development, and education
Innovation
Cutting-edge, evidence-based approaches
Social Justice
Equality, diversity, and inclusion at the core
Collaboration
Partners around the world, stronger together
Impact
Education as a force for meaningful social change
